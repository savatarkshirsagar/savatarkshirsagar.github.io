About Us

Our Company Values are based on Dedication, Respect for our clients investment and a constant drive to achieve the final goal. Together with our Clients, we grow and learn,with complete and unparalleled Dedication we provide comprehensive service to our client and strive to maximize our clients return to investment.With our professional team of consultants and our conveniently located offices we at SRK groups are able to assist several clients every month on all type of Real Estate Transactions. Focusing on Sales, Rentals and Property Management we strive to meet the ever growing demand of our Clients. Investing in our Sales Team and maintaining our first class reputation has given us a competitive edge and has allowed us to excel in one of the worlds most competitive Real Estate market. We continue to strive for Excellence.

A delight spreading to over 2,000 happy families in Latur, over 1.4 million sq.ft. of state-of-the-art and futuristic development, all are clear pointers towards the friendly customer base in Latur. And while our Customers are Kings for us, Nature is our Mother. Till date we have carried forward the rich heritage of eco-friendliness. With the second generation at the helm that's armed with futuristic vision and ambitious growth plans the company is all set to take the next leap.
